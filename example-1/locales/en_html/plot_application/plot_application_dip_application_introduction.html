Overview

Use this form to tell us how you intend to pay for the Self Build Wales loan and the Land Purchase Deposit.

The Self Build Wales team will use the information you provide in this form to assess the affordability of your loan.

Before you begin

You can save your progress on any page of your application allowing you to log back in to complete the form when it suits you.

You will need to have ready the following information:

  • For any joint applicants, their full name, date of birth and address
  • For any properties owned by the applicants you will need the address, outstanding mortgage amount and estimated property sale value.
  • If you plan to use any savings to repay the loan then you need to tell us the total amount.
  • If you plan to get a new mortgage, you need to tell us the estimated amount.
  • You will need to confirm that you have an immediate source of funding to pay for the Land Purchase deposit.
