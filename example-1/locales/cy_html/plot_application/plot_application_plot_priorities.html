Application criteria

There are specific criterion applicable to this plot, detailed below, and priority will be given to applicants who meet all, or most, of these requirements:

  • Families seeking adaptable housing
  • Young adults with additional needs requiring in-house care and support services
  • Higher energy efficiency proposals

Performance requirements

Applicants are encouraged to implement the highest possible energy efficiencies for their home.

Some current methods of construction are not yet recognised by mortgage providers, and applicants wishing to use non-standard construction methods should check the ability to refinance the self-build development loan with lenders before applying for a plot.

Build duration

The dwelling must be completed (and the self-build development loan repaid) within 24 months of the plot purchase.

Plot boundaries

Planning permission states that a variety of trees, up to a maximum of 3.5 metres in height, are to be planted along the rear boundary of the plot. Hedgerows are to be planted along the length of the right-hand boundary line.

Additional information

Additional car parking is not permissible outside of the spaces approved within the boundaries of the development site.
